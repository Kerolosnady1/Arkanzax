The 7 KPIs Every Marketing Team Must Track

Feb 10, 2025 Data Team Strategy

Data is only valuable if you know what to look for. In a world drowning in metrics, focusing on the right KPIs is the difference between success and failure.

1. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

How much are you actually spending to get a new customer? This metric must be balanced against lifetime value to ensure long-term profitability.

2. Conversion Rate by Channel

Not all traffic is created equal. Understanding which platforms provide the highest quality leads allows you to optimize your budget allocation.

3. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

The total revenue you can expect from a single customer account. Increasing CLV is often more cost-effective than acquiring new customers.
